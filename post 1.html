"Creamy Homemade Vanilla Pudding: A Spoonful of Comfort"

12'th Feb 2023

Hey there, fellow food enthusiasts! Today, I'm thrilled to share a delightful recipe that has a special place in my heart – homemade vanilla pudding. There's something incredibly comforting about a bowl of velvety, made-from-scratch pudding, and the best part? It's surprisingly easy to whip up. So, let's dive into the world of creamy goodness!


Ingredients


• 2 cups whole milk • 1/2 cup granulated sugar • 1/4 cup cornstarch • 1/4 teaspoon salt • 4 large egg yolks • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract


Prepare Your Ingredients


Before we start, ensure all your ingredients are at room temperature. Separate the egg yolks from the whites, and set them aside. In a medium-sized saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt. This ensures our pudding will be smooth without any lumps. Place the saucepan over medium heat and gradually whisk in the whole milk. Stir continuously until the mixture starts to simmer. We don't want it to boil, just a gentle simmer will do.


Combine and Cook


In a separate bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks. Gradually add a small amount of the hot milk mixture to the eggs while whisking continuously. This "tempers" the eggs, preventing them from scrambling when added to the hot milk. Pour the egg mixture back into the saucepan with the rest of the milk mixture. Keep stirring over medium heat until the pudding thickens. This usually takes about 2-3 minutes Once the pudding has thickened, remove it from the heat. Stir in the unsalted butter and pure vanilla extract. This is where the magic happens – the aroma is simply irresistible!


Chill and Serve


For an extra-smooth texture, you can strain the pudding through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. This step is optional, but it does make the pudding incredibly silky. Cover the pudding with plastic wrap, ensuring it touches the surface to prevent a skin from forming. Pop it in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or until it's nicely chilled. Grab your favorite bowls or cute dessert glasses, spoon in that luscious vanilla pudding, and savor every creamy bite. It's the perfect treat for any occasion!
